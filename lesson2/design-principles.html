Hick's Law

LeetCode

leetcode.com

The leetcode page shows the button to create an account in the middle of its content, which complies with Hick's law, which teaches us that the buttons or sections that are a call to action must be easily located for the final user

White Space and Clean Design

Shopify

shopify.com

The spotify page is a good example of White Space and Clean Design since by keeping light and white tones it gives the impression of cleanliness and order, they also show many blank areas that divide each area very well in order to make our navigation easier

Contrast

Jungle Scout

junglescout.com

It seems to me that the page of JungleScout has some good colors without losing the contrast between letters and other elements. keeping the squares white allows you to leave the background behind for easy-to-read content
