Target Audience

The target is for a group of the population who have businesses in the Caracas area and need to create a network of acquaintances who are also in the commercial field or who seek to give and receive support from others in the commercial sector.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

person who heard about the chamber of commerce and would like to know more about it this person does not have a business and does not just want to receive information.

Scenario 2

Person who owns a business interested in being part of the chamber of commerce, this person can find entry forms on the page.

Scenario 3

A person who is already part of the chamber is looking for specific information regarding data update services, the page provides a calendar with future service dates.

Scenario 4

Company owner who wants to find a person or business to help her out of a common problem that she does not know how to solve, on the page you can find contact information for other people and companies

Scenario 5

The manager of a company needs information about the laws that apply to his business.
